Personal Web Project: Milestone 1

Purpose

I would like for my PWP to be the potential about-me page for my professional website. It will contain a brief
explanation of my personal and professional history, skills and interests while showcasing the languages I've
learned in this program. From this bootcamp I hope to pursue web development and UI/UX design, as part of my
background is in the visual arts. Because of that, at some point I would also like to have a part of my website
devoted to either selling or offering commissions, as well as displaying other creative, nonprofessional ventures
I decide to follow (blog, .

Audience

Potential clients and those I am able to connect with via networking will be viewing my site, along with others just
wishing to view my art. My intended audience is made up of those in the technology field and creatives. I hope that my
peers would be able to appreciate it as well as I further my networking.

Goal

I hope for my website to give me a platform to both express myself while showcasing the technical and desirable job
skills I have to solve client solutions. I hope for it to gain me both clients and a reputation for integrity in what
I do. I hope that through my work I am able to solidify myself as an integral part of my community and the industry,
while also being able to explore my creativity.

Persona

Francesca Worthman

  • Age: 34
  • Location: Albuquerque
  • Occupation: Coffee Shop Owner and Roaster
  • Technology: Google Chrome, iPhone 12, MacBook Pro
  • Wants to build community engagement as a local business and sought-out destination for both locals and tourists
  • Wishes to uphold high standards of product while also being accessible to those unfamiliar with coffee

Attitudes and Behaviors

She keeps up with personal and business accounts on social media for marketing, but has been needing a properly done
website to enable selling merchandise online and building a customer base. She knows that having a website would be
great for her business, but she often finds her time devoted to roasting the beans and ensuring the business runs smoothly.

Frustrations and Needs

She is finding it difficult to stay competitive in the growing coffee industry here in the city, especially in appealing
to younger customers and coffee lovers. She needs better marketing and visibility.

Goals

She would like to solidify herself as a member of the coffee community and Albuquerque itself. She wants to alleviate
the stresses of marketing with a well-done website so she can focus on making a good product.

Francesca would use my site to understand my style and artistic choices to decide if I could do what she would
like on her platform. She would be able to view my portfolio and learn more about me to determine if she would like to work together.

I would work with Francesca to not only create an engaging website, but also help her define her brand
to help the design of the site be as cohesive and user-friendly as possible. I would make an interface that is
graphically beautiful while also being easy with which to navigate ecommerce for her products.

User Story

"As a business owner and entrepreneur, I would like to have a website to help me grow my business, strengthen my
connections in the coffee industry, and become a greater asset to my community."
