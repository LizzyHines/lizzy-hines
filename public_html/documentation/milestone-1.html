Personal Web Project: Milestone 1

Purpose

I would like for my PWP to be the potential about-me page for my professional website. It will contain a brief
explanation of my personal and professional history, skills and interests while showcasing the languages I've
learned in this program. From this bootcamp I hope to pursue web development and UI/UX design, as part of my
background is in the visual arts. Because of that, at some point I would also like to have a part of my website
devoted to either selling or offering commissions, as well as displaying other creative, nonprofessional ventures
I decide to follow.

Audience

Potential clients and those I am able to connect with via networking will be viewing my site, along with others just
wishing to view my art. My audience will be made up of those in the technology industry and creatives. I hope that my
peers would be able to appreciate it as well as I further my networking.

Goal

I hope for my website to give me a platform to both express myself while showcasing the technical and desirable job
skills I have to solve client solutions. I hope for it to gain me both clients and a reputation for integrity in what
I do. I hope that through my work I am able to solidify myself as an integral part of my community and the design industry.
