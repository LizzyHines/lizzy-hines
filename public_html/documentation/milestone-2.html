Content Strategy

My website will be one page, and will consist of the following elements:

Navigation Bar

A navigation bar at the top of the page will navigate people to either the About, Skills, or Contact section of the page.

Welcome/About Section

A photo of me in the Bosque will span the frame, and a text frame will contain my name and my role as an aspiring
developer and designer. Below this will be a band of color containing several sentences about my background in visual arts and the
humanities, and what has led me to pursue a career in computer programming.

Skills Section

Below the color bar with my biography will be a short synopsis of the skills I have in programming and in visual media. This will be within a bordered section of text with icons depicting each specific skill. In the margins of the page will be the background image of an original piece I created.

Contact Section

This section will have space for anyone wishing to contact me to leave their contact information and a message they want to send. The background piece in the Skills section will continue into the background of this section.
