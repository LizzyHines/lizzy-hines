Content Strategy

My website will be one page and have the following sections:

Navigation Bar

A navigation bar at the top of the page will navigate people to the About, Skills, or Contact section of the page.

Welcome/About Section

A photo of me in the Bosque will span the frame, and a text frame will contain my name and my role as an aspiring developer and
designer. Below this will be a band of color containing several sentences about my background in visual arts and the humanities, and
what has led me to pursue a career in computer programming.

Skills and Portfolio Section

Below the color bar with my biography will be a short synopsis of the skills I have in programming with icons for each skill or
language. This will be accompanied by a portfolio section, with links to multiple projects' webpages and a brief summary of each project.
In the margins of the page will be the background image of an original piece I created.

Contact Section

This section will have space for anyone wishing to contact me to see my information or use the space to leave their contact information
and a message they want to send. The background piece in the Skills section will continue into the background of this section.
